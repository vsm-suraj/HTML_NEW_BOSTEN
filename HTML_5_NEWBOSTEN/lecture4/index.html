MSI Brings the eSports Experience Back to PAX East

[Taipei, Taiwan] MSI, a world leader in gaming hardware, announces the return of the MSI eSports experience at PAX East in Boston, MA from April 5 to April 8. Attendees will have the opportunity to experience MSI products hands-on and compete with other attendees to win prizes and other MSI swag. MSI's PAX East experience will be headlined by the showcase of the brand new GS65 Stealth Thin gaming laptop. "As the eSports industry continues to evolve, MSI is the only true gaming brand and has been at the forefront of gaming innovations," said Sam Chern, MSI Global Marketing Asssistant Vice President. "At PAX East, we look forward to indulge gamers' cravings with our latest gaming machines."
